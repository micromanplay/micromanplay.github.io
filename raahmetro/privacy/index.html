RaahMetro — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

RaahMetro ("the app", "we", "us") is an unofficial, community-built metro navigation app for Indian metro networks. This policy explains what data the app uses, what is and is not collected, and your choices. The app is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to DMRC or any metro operator.

Summary (TL;DR)

  • The app's core features work on your device. Route planning, fares, timings and the network map are computed locally from data bundled with the app.
  • We do not run our own accounts, login, or analytics servers, and we do not sell your personal data.
  • The app shows ads through Google AdMob, and it may use your location on your device. These are explained below.

1. Location

The app can use your device location for two on-device purposes:

  • Detecting your metro city and showing the nearest stations (ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION / ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION). This computation happens on your device. Your precise location is not sent to any RaahMetro server (we do not operate one for this).
  • Location permission is optional. If you decline, you can still use the app by selecting your city manually.
Note: see Section 3 — the Google AdMob SDK may independently use coarse location signals to serve ads, governed by Google's own privacy policy.

2. Data Stored on Your Device

The app stores the following locally, never transmitted to us:

  • Your selected city, theme and language preferences.
  • Saved/recent routes and the home-screen Quick Route widget data.
  • A local cache of bundled metro data.

You can clear locally cached data anytime from Settings → Data & Storage → Clear cache.

3. Advertising (Google AdMob)

The app displays ads using Google AdMob (react-native-google-mobile-ads). To serve ads, the Google Mobile Ads SDK may collect and process:

  • An advertising identifier (Android Advertising ID),
  • Device and usage information (e.g. device model, OS version, IP address, ad interactions),
  • Approximate location derived from the above signals.

This data is collected and used by Google, acting as an independent controller, under Google's policies:

  • Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • How Google uses information from sites/apps that use its services: https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites
  • AdMob/Ads: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

You can limit ad personalization via your device settings (Settings → Google → Ads on Android, e.g. "Reset advertising ID" or "Opt out of Ads Personalization").

4. Third-Party Services

  • Google AdMob — advertising (see Section 3).
  • OpenStreetMap — the bundled metro dataset is derived from OpenStreetMap (© OpenStreetMap contributors, ODbL). Map data is bundled; the app does not require you to log in to any map service.
  • Telegram Bot (optional) — if you use the separate RaahMetro Telegram bot, your interaction is subject to Telegram's privacy policy; the bot processes your messages to answer metro queries and does not store personal profiles.

5. Children

The app is a general-audience utility and is not directed at children. It does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Data Security & Retention

Because the app's core data lives on your device, you control it — uninstalling the app or clearing its cache/storage removes locally stored preferences and cached data. Ad-related data handling is governed by Google (Section 3).

7. Your Rights (incl. India DPDP Act, 2023)

We aim to align with India's Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Act, 2023. Since the app itself does not collect personal data on our servers, most data remains under your control on your device. For ad-related data, exercise your rights through Google's controls (Section 3) and device-level ad settings.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Material changes will be reflected here with a new "Last updated" date.
